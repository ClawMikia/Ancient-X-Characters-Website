Creator

The Architect

World Builder · Narrative Designer

The mind behind Ancient X is a storyteller obsessed with the space where mythology collides with human vulnerability. Inspired by classic wuxia literature, dark fantasy anime, and the soul-crushing narrative weight of games like Dark Souls and Final Fantasy XIV, the creator set out to build a world that rewards curiosity and punishes complacency.

Ancient X began as a sketchbook of character concepts and spiraled into a fully-realized universe with three continents, seventeen noble bloodlines, and a cosmology that rewrites the rules of life and death every few hundred years.

"I don't write heroes," the creator has said. "I write people standing in impossible places, choosing who they want to become before the dark decides for them."
